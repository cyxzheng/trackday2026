May 4, 2026

A Message from Supreme Leader

Avatar Say Phin Lim

Hi everybody.

I just want to take this time to give my heartfelt thanks to all the YVRGRC members for taking the time out of your busy lives to come out to our GR After Hours event. This really shows how tight knit our GR community has become.

As we look to the horizon, it is with deep sorrow that I must share my departure from my position at OpenRoad Toyota Richmond, with my last day being May 4, 2026. This tenure has been some of the most fulfilling in my career, building my team internally and also making a lot of new friends along the way with YVRGRC.

Alas, it is time to say goodbye but I am still a proud member of the GRC community and will continue to grow the club as much as I can with the admin team. I am leaving the club in good hands with several club members still at the dealership in key positions in parts, service, techs and sales department so I've been assured that there will be no disruption to our club perks.

See you all soon in our regular club meets!

- Say
